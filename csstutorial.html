How to sew a trouser

Here is a quick guidelines on how to cut a trouser:

  1. Get materials like;
    • Lace
    • Ankara
    • Ginni
    • Plain materials
    • Pattern materials
    NB:Plain materials & pattern materials are most preferable in making trousers
  2. Get a cutting table ready with your cutting materials
    • scisors
    • Tape rule
    • Chalk
    • Ruler
  3. Fold the material to be use once and place it on the table;
    make sure the length and the width suit the measurement you are working toward
  4. Iron the material for conviency while drawing pattern
  5. Measure and mark the waist of the cloth at the horizontal edge of the material and add 2 inches to the original length seem allowance
