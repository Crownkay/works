ABOUT CROWNKAY WEARS



by Adekola:

CROWNKAY was originated from a yoruba word "Adekola" in the year 2017. The name of the founder and CEO of the company Adeniran Adekola Moses. He was born in the city of Ogbomoso, Oyo State, Nigeria. He went to Ladoke Akintola University of Technology where he studied Statistics and obtain his B.Tech Degree Award.
